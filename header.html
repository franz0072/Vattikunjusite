    Short Intro

    Welcome to St. Michael's Sr. Sec School, Vatika Kunj, Gurugram, one of the finest english medium school in Gurugram

    St. Michael's School, Vatika Kunj, Gurugram is a Catholic School owned and administered by Delhi Catholic Archdiocese, a religious, charitable, educational and social organization registered under the Societies Registration Act XXX of 1860 no. S-512 of 1951-52. One of the core interventions of Delhi Catholic Archdiocese is imparting 'Quality and Value Education for All' in the Capital Region and in Haryana. Therefore, Delhi Catholic Archdiocese established St. Michael's School at Vatika Kunj in 2006. It is an unaided Christian Minority school. It admits eligible children of all communities with a special responsibility of imparting education with commitment. Initially, the Sisters of the Congregation of Lady of Mercy administered the school; they also took care of the Alan de Lastic Memorial Village situated in the same campus. However, a diocesan priest functioned as the manager. Rev. Fr. Thavamani was appointed as the first priest principal of St. Michael School in 2015. Under his able supervision, the present structure of the school building was completed and His Grace Most Rev Anil J T Cuto, Archbishop of Delhi, inaugurated the school in 2018. Since 2017, the Sisters of the Congregation of Immaculate Conception collaborate with the Archdiocese of Delhi in the imparting of education at St. Michael's School.

    SCHOOL FACILITIES

    Holistic Education with Modern Infrastructure

    Education provided by our school goes beyond the limits of text books. Students are exposed to different National Scholarship Exams and Inter school and other competitions. They are given several opportunities to develop their talents. The cultural activities are framed to build up stage confidence and inculcate social and moral values among young ones. Physical training is a part of the regular programme in the school schedule. Sports & Games competitions are conducted every year, for all the students under four houses and the best house is awarded with ever rolling trophy. Training and practice for games like Basketball, Football, Kabaddy, Khokho, Hockey, and Cricket are given to the students.
